Built for four kinds of users

Every account on Alumora belongs to one role, with its own dashboard and permissions.

Students

Browse alumni, send mentorship requests and track sessions.

Alumni

Accept mentorship requests and guide students once verified.

Faculty

Approve mentorship requests and send notices to students.

University admins

Manage their own university's accounts and requests.

Every mentorship is tracked end to end

A request moves through three states — nothing is left open-ended.

PENDING
Request sent

A student requests mentorship from an alumnus.

ACCEPTED
Mentorship active

The alumnus accepts and sessions can begin.

COMPLETED
Marked complete

Either side closes it out once the mentorship ends.
